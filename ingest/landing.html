how actually-for-you works

  1. capturea chrome extension records the posts i see and a small set of reading signals.
  2. ranka local node and sqlite service scores candidates without sending my history to a hosted database.
  3. reviewevery digest card has keep and drop controls. those hand votes are the only gold labels.
  4. testnew rankers must clear an offline gate, then earn their result in a blinded live comparison.
